## 🚨 Problem Solved

Companies are adopting **AI tools**, **model providers**, **memory layers**, **agentic runtimes**, **workflow automations**, and **vendor platforms** before leadership can prove:

- **who authorized the dependency**
- **what it retains**
- **where memory or data crosses jurisdiction**
- **what consequence it can bind**
- **whether access can be revoked**
- **whether the organization can exit without operational failure**
- **whether the review decision can be replayed later**

This creates **board exposure**, **regulatory exposure**, **cyber exposure**, **audit failure**, **vendor lock-in**, and **structural dependency without approved authority**.
